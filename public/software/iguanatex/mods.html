Modifications to IguanaTex

The following links point to modifications and extensions of IguanaTex, performed by enthusiastic users.

I'm extremely grateful to everyone for their work on this, but I am not taking responsibility for this work. In other words, I'm providing these links for information only.

Resizeable IguanaTex window

Hammad M has written a short article on how to make the IguanaTex editor window resizeable.

Keep equations properly resized after recompiling

Jonathan Le Roux has a modified version of IguanaTex which allows you to resize an equation and then modify its content, while correctly maintaining the new image size and aspect ratio.

Jonathan's change also includes the resizeable IguanaTex window written by Hammad M.
